What you need

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • A Pot

What to do!

  1. Prepare the water, ensure there is plenty of water (more water then pasta)
  2. Cook the pasta, make sure to stir regularly to so it doesnt stick to the bottom of the pot
  3. I like to taste test to see if its cooked or not
  4. Drain and serve however you'd like
